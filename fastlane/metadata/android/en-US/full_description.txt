SoundPod is a minimalist, open-source music player. Built with Jetpack Compose, it offers a modern interface for streaming media using publicly accessible APIs.

Key Features:
• Background playback and persistent queue management.
• High-quality caching for offline playback.
• Comprehensive search for songs, albums, artists, and playlists.
• Bookmark your favorite artists and albums for quick access.
• Import and manage local and remote playlists.
• Fetch, display, and edit synchronized or static lyrics.
• Seamlessly opens YouTube and YouTube Music links.
• Smart features: Sleep timer, skip silence, and audio normalization.
• Ecosystem support: Full Android Auto integration.
• Invincible service to ensure uninterrupted background listening.
